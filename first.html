Upto 28years

Upto 28years (used from location 1)

  • Ask only once to yourself.
  • Now that you know, hold your thought.

  • Let the thought play, keep a check on it.
  • Thought can be pictures or sounds/voices or all of it. It can be as real as reality.
  • Check if same thought is repeating or it changes with minor variations.

  • If it bothers you, then make slight variations in the same thought.
  • Start with very small change of unthought eg. If your thought is to break anything completely, now, think of breaking it a little and not completely.
  • Again think of breaking it less than last time.
  • Again think of breaking it lesser than last time.
  • Again think of breaking it much lesser than last time.
  • Again think of, breaking it very little that it is not visible or it cannot be felt.
  • Now, leave the thought as it is and consider it complete.

  • If you find unthought is useless, then wait.
  • Wait and do not act on your thought.
  • While waiting, don’t shiver and don’t get impatient.
  • Wait only as much as you can but wait 5secs more than you want to.

  • Do you feel you can support yourself then do the unthought again.
  • This time it will help you little more than last time.
  • If you need my support then you know where to reach me. Do it now.
